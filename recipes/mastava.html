Mastava

Description

Mastava is a traditional Uzbek soup made from rice, vegetables, and meat. It's a hearty and flavorful dish often enjoyed as a comforting meal during colder months. The rich flavors of the broth combined with tender meat and fresh vegetables make it a favorite in Central Asian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 200g beef or lamb
  • 100g rice
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 potatoes, cubed
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 2 liters water
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh dill for garnish

Steps

  1. In a pot, heat oil and brown the meat.
  2. Add the onions and cook until softened.
  3. Mix in the carrots and tomatoes, then cook for another 5 minutes.
  4. Add the water, bring to a boil, and simmer for 30 minutes.
  5. Stir in the rice and potatoes, and cook until both are tender, about 20 minutes.
  6. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Garnish with fresh dill before serving.
