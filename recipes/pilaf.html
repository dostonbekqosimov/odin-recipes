Pilaf

Description

Palov, also known as Plov, is a traditional Uzbek rice dish cooked with meat, onions, carrots, and spices. It is a staple in Central Asia and is often served on special occasions. The dish's rich flavors and colorful presentation make it a crowd favorite.

Ingredients

  • 500g beef or lamb
  • 500g long-grain rice
  • 3 carrots, julienned
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 100ml vegetable oil
  • 1 head of garlic
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 2 liters water

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a large pot and brown the meat.
  2. Add the onions and cook until golden brown.
  3. Add the carrots and cook for another 5 minutes.
  4. Pour in the water, add cumin, salt, and pepper, and bring to a boil.
  5. Place the garlic in the center, reduce heat, and simmer for 30 minutes.
  6. Add the rice and smooth it out evenly on top of the meat and vegetables.
  7. Cover and cook until the rice is fully cooked, about 20 minutes.
